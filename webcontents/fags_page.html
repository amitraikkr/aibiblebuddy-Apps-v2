What is AI Bible Assistant for Kids?

AI Bible Assistant for Kids is an interactive mobile app designed to help children learn about the Bible in a fun and engaging way.

You can reset your password by going to the login page and tapping on 'Forgot Password?'. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your data security. We encrypt all personal information and do not share it with third parties.

How can I contact support?

You can contact support by emailing us at support@aibiblebuddy.com or by using the contact form within the app.

Can I use the app without an internet connection?

Some features of the app require an internet connection, but many stories and activities can be accessed offline once downloaded.
